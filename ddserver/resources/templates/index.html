Welcome to ddserver

Good news everyone! As of 21.12.2013 Magrathea Laboratories is running its own dynamic DNS service called ddserver.

The service allows you to specify hostnames inside our dynamic zones that can be updated using a standard homerouter or the ddclient software.

If you are a member of Magrathea Laboratories you may create an account using the button on the right. If you are some random human, who wants to use this service, you may also create an account (at the moment).

Fees

This service is provided for free now and in the future. Donations are very welcome. We reserve the right, to close registration for external users, but, you can always join our hackerspace, Magrathea Laboratories :)
